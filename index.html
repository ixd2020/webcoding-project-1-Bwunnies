More About His Artworks

Robert Delaunay was born in Paris, the son of George Delaunay and Countess Berthe Félicie de Rose. While he was a child, Delaunay's parents divorced, and he was raised by his mother's sister Marie and her husband Charles Damour, in La Ronchère near Bourges. style="float": right; margin-left: 20px; margin-bottom:5px;

In his Windows series, a group of twenty-two paintings made between April and December of 1912, Delaunay rejected painting’s traditional function as a window onto an imaginary world....
